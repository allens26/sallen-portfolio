About Me

I have eight years of experience as a K-12 teacher in all subject areas, although my focus has been on grades 4-8. In my time at the iSchool, I focused on school librarianship -- specifically at the secondary level -- as well as on information access and the intersection of social justice and young adult literature. I have a particular interest in young adult books about mental health, feminism, and LGBTQIA+ issues.

I have a B.A. in History/Social Studies from Western Washington University and an MLIS from the University of Washington. My teaching endorsements are in 4-9 Humanities, 5-12 Social Studies, and Library Media.
